Privacy Policy for WorldTrendz

WorldTrendz respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

When you use our application, we may collect the following information:

  • Email address and account information (for authentication)

  • Messages and interactions within the app (such as AI chat features)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Firebase (authentication and database services)

These services may process your data only as necessary to provide their functionality.

Payments

All purchases are processed through external Shopify checkout pages. We do not collect or store payment information within the app.

Notifications

The app may request permission to send notifications. If enabled, we may use a device push token to deliver notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with third-party services as required to operate the app.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data and maintain the security of our services.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data directly from within the app.
